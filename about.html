Who I Am

I’m a third-year Mechanical Engineering student at the University of Alberta with a strong passion for design, innovation, and problem-solving. Growing up I always had an interest in designing things, whether it be drawing, modeling, or building things out of lego, I could never get enough of it. I decided to pursue an engineering degree so that I can take my love of creativity, and my love for math, to the professional level.

Hobbies

My hobbies are drawing, pottery, and playing musical instruments. I play 7 instruments: Harp, Piano, Flute, Ukulele, Guitar, Piccolo, and I sing. I've always enjoyed designing and creating and my dream is to combine that passion with engineering work.
